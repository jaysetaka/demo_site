Introduction

Wonder & Family Projects, a Pty Ltd duly incorporated in terms of the company laws of South Africa, under the registration number (K2016479803), is a black empowerment company founded in 2015 and worked the entire of 2015 learning and perfecting the crafts of the industry the registered in 2016 as a specialist property maintenance, project management and property development company.

A strong emphasis is placed on our customer’s needs and a wide variety of services offered to our clients. Our client base include Government, Corporate and Private Sector Clients.
